The Rules

Green | You start the levels on this color and you have to finish on the other one
Pink | No effect, can be passed through
Red | Blocks movement, acts as a wall
Blue | If the player's scent is Orange, acts as a wall. Else, can be passed through
Purple | Teleport tile, makes the player slide to the next tile, or bounce if the next tile is a wall, also changes the player's scent to Lemon
Orange | Changes the player's scent to Orange
